Account & Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and all associated data at any time. We take your privacy seriously and process all requests promptly.

How to request account deletion

Send an email to the address below and we'll process your request within 7 days.

nick@competeng.com

Please include in your email:

  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • The email address associated with your account

What data will be deleted

  • Account profile information
  • Activity and streak data
  • Weekly goal history
  • Team and partner connections
  • Uploaded content (if applicable)

Deletion timeframe & retention

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days of receipt.

No personal data is retained after deletion, except where required by law.
